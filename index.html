AlNoor Electric Store

Description

Alnoor Electric Store is a retail store that specializes in selling a wide variety of electrical appliances and accessories. They offer products such as refrigerators, washing machines, air conditioners, fans, heaters, and more. The store also carries electrical accessories like switches, plugs, sockets, and lighting fixtures. Alnoor Electric Store is known for offering high-quality products from top brands at competitive prices. They also provide excellent customer service, with knowledgeable and helpful staff who can assist customers in choosing the right product for their needs. The store's commitment to quality and customer satisfaction has made it a popular choice for those looking to purchase electrical appliances and accessories in the local area.

Selling Items In Our Store

  • Electrical wires and cables: Copper wires, Coaxial cables, Ethernet cables, and many more.
  • Electrical components: Circuit breakers, Switches, Fuses, Outlets, and Receptacles
  • Lighting fixtures: Light bulbs, LED lights, Fluorescent lights, Chandeliers, and Track lighting.
  • Electrical tools: Pliers, Screwdrivers, Wire strippers, Wire cutters, Cable testers, Multimeters, and Voltage detectors.
  • Safety equipment: Gloves, Hard hats, Safety glasses, and Protective clothing.
  • Electrical tape: Used to insulate and bind electrical wires and cables.
  • Conduit and tubing: PVC, EMT and Flexible Conduit for organizing and protecting wires.
  • Electrical boxes and enclosures: Metal and Plastic enclosures for indoor and outdoor use, for distribution, and storage of electrical equipment.
  • Heating and cooling devices: Fans, Heaters, and Air conditioners.
  • Generators: Portable and Standby generators for power backup in homes, businesses, and industrial sites.
