About us

Welcome to AlNoor Electrical store, your one-stop-shop for all your electrical needs. We have been serving the Gajjumatah (Lahore) community for 03 years and have built a reputation for providing high-quality electrical products and exceptional customer service.
Our store offers a wide selection of electrical equipment, from wiring and circuit breakers to lighting fixtures and tools. Our team of experienced electricians is available to answer any questions you may have and help you find the products you need for your next project.
At AlNoor electrical Store, we believe in providing our customers with the best possible experience. That's why we offer competitive pricing, fast shipping, and a hassle-free return policy. Our goal is to make your shopping experience as easy and convenient as possible.
We also understand the importance of safety when it comes to electrical work. That's why we only carry products from reputable brands and manufacturers that meet industry safety standards.
Whether you're a professional electrician or a DIY enthusiast, AlNoor Electrical Store has everything you need to get the job done right. Thank you for choosing us as your go-to source for all your electrical needs.
